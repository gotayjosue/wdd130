Home page

The homepage of my website will be the most atractive part of it. I'm thinking about put in there the best images of Trujillo I have and make it with a very good look and maybe with some animations and transitions for the images (an animated gallery).

Places

This page is called places because I am going to put here the different places people can visit in Trujillo city. It's going to be like link images with animations. When people click on one image it's going to redirect to another page where is going to be a brief description of the place and it's history.
